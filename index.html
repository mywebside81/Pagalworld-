Ek Khoobsurat Safar...

Waqt kitni jaldi guzar jaata hai, pata hi nahi chala. Aisa lagta hai jaise kal ki hi baat ho jab hum pehli baar mile the. In 3 saalon mein humne saath mein hasa, sapne dekhe, aur ek doosre ka har pal saath diya. Yeh website hamari unhi yaadon ko samarpit hai.

Hamari Yaadon ki Galiyan

Pehla Saal: Jab Hum Mile

Ek anjaan shuruaat se lekar ek doosre ki aadat banne tak ka safar. Woh pehli mulakat, woh lambi baatein, sab kuch kitna khaas tha.

Doosra Saal: Yaadein Banti Gayi

Humne saath mein kitne pal bitaaye. Hamari pehli trip, choti-choti ladaiyan aur dher saara pyaar. Har din ek nayi memory ban rahi thi.

Teesra Saal: Aaj aur Hamesha

Aaj humara rishta aur bhi gehra aur mazboot ho gaya hai. Har mushkil mein ek doosre ka sahara bane, aur har khushi saath manayi. Aane wale kal ke liye dher saare sapne hain.
